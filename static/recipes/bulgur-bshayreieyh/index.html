Bulgur BShayreieyh

Toasted vermicelli noodles mixed with coarse bulgur. A rustic and hearty side dish traditionally served with Okra stew (Bamia) or yogurt-based dishes.

⏲️ Time: 45 minutes
🍽️ Servings: The quantity is enough for 5 people.
📂 Category: Grain Dishes

🥄 Ingredients

  • 1 kg coarse white bulgur
  • 1 cup vermicelli
  • 3 tablespoons ghee
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 4 cups boiling water

👩‍🍳 Instructions

Put the ghee in a suitable pot and heat it, then add the vermicelli and toast it until it turns golden. Add the bulgur and toast it for 5 minutes, then add the boiling water and let it boil again. Cover it and wait until the water level drops below the bulgur, then reduce the heat to a quarter and leave it for about 10 minutes or until the water dries up.

✨ Chef's Secret

This dish goes great with all yogurt dishes and okra with meat.

🌹 Decoration

Garnish the bulgur and vermicelli dish with pickle slices and green cucumber.
