Sheikh al-Mahshi Bakar

Golden fried zucchini rounds topped with a savory layer of spiced minced meat and fresh parsley. A delicious and practical alternative to stuffed zucchini, served with a refreshing side of cold yogurt and garlic.

⏲️ Time: 1.5 hours
🍽️ Servings: 6 people

🥄 Ingredients

  • 750g lean minced meat
  • 1/2 bunch chopped parsley (optional)
  • 2 medium onions (350g) (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp seven-spice mix
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp ghee for cooking the meat
  • 100g pine nuts (if available)

For the zucchini

  • 2 kg large zucchini (depending on the season)
  • 1 kg full-fat yogurt
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp dried mint
  • Oil for frying the zucchini

👩‍🍳 Instructions

Meat Preparation

Chop the onion and sauté it in a tablespoon of ghee or oil until softened. Add the meat and cook, breaking it up into pieces. Cover and cook over low heat for 1.5 hour. Add the seven-spice mix, black pepper, salt, and pine nuts.

Zucchini Preparation: Wash the zucchini thoroughly to remove any dirt. Cut it into thick slices about 3 cm thick. Once the oil is hot, fry the zucchini slices until golden brown, not dark brown.

After frying, add the zucchini to the meat you prepared earlier. Add a cup of boiling water, salt, and black pepper. Let it simmer over medium heat for about 15 minutes.

Yogurt Sauce Preparation: In a deep bowl, combine the yogurt, garlic, and mint. Mix well until smooth. Serve cold with rice and the zucchini slices.

🌹 Decoration

Sprinkle dried mint or chopped parsley on top.
