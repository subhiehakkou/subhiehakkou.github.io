Green Okra in oil

Tender okra pods sautéed with tomatoes, onions, and garlic in extra virgin olive oil. A flavorful vegan dish often finished with a touch of dried mint or cilantro, served with pita bread.

⏲️ Time: 1 hour
🍽️ Servings: The quantity is enough for 5 people.

🥄 Ingredients

  • 1 kg fresh or frozen okra
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 0.5 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 1 cup good quality olive oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions

Clean the okra if it's fresh and cut it into your desired size. If it's frozen, soak it in boiling water immediately. Don't remove it from the freezer beforehand; put it directly into the boiling water.

Heat olive oil in a suitable pot. Add the garlic and sauté until golden brown. Then add the dried coriander and sauté with the garlic for 2-3 minutes. Add the okra and sauté for 5 minutes. At this point, add the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes (not too large or too small), salt, and a cup of boiling water. Stir well, cover the pot, and simmer over low heat for 30 minutes, or until the okra is tender.

✨ Chef's Secret

This dish is very nutritious for vegetarians.

🌹 Decoration

Garnish the okra dish with green peppers.
