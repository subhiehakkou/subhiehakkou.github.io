mayonnaise

A smooth and creamy emulsion made from fresh egg yolks, corn or sunflower oil, and a touch of lemon juice. Using these light oils ensures a perfect velvety texture and a clean, fresh taste without the heaviness of other vegetable oils.

⏲️ Time: 15 minutes

🥄 Ingredients

  • 1 cup Mazola corn oil or sunflower oil only
  • 1 raw egg
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 0.25 teaspoon salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
  • 0.25 teaspoon white pepper

👩‍🍳 Instructions

Hold the hand blender and fix it in the bottom of the cup or container with the oil without moving your hand. Turn it on at a low speed for a full minute. You will notice that the oil has started to solidify at the bottom. At this stage, gradually and slowly raise your hand upwards until you reach the top of the oil. Then start moving slowly from top to bottom and from bottom to top until the oil has completely solidified. This is how you get healthy homemade mayonnaise without preservatives and fresh for use in coleslaw.

✨ Chef's Secret

Try making mayonnaise at home just once and you won't buy it after that.
