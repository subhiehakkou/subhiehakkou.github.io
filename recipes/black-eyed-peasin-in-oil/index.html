black-eyed peasin in oil

Fresh black-eyed peasslow-cooked with tomatoes, onions, and garlic in extra virgin olive oil. A healthy and traditional vegan dish served cold or warm with pita bread.

⏲️ Time: 1 hour
🍽️ Servings: The quantity is enough for 5 people.

🥄 Ingredients

  • 1 kg green beans (fresh or frozen)
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 0.5 tablespoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 4 medium tomatoes
  • 1 cup good quality olive oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Clean the green beans if they are fresh and cut them to your desired size. If they are frozen, soak them in boiling water immediately. Don't remove them from the freezer beforehand; transfer them directly from the freezer to the boiling water.
  2. Heat olive oil in a suitable pot. Add the garlic and sauté until golden brown. Then add the dried coriander and sauté with the garlic for 2-3 minutes. Add the green beans and sauté for 5 minutes. At this point, add the tomato paste, chopped tomatoes (not too large or too small), salt, and a cup of boiling water. Stir well, cover the pot, and simmer over low heat for 30 minutes, or until the green beans are tender.

✨ Chef's Secret

This dish is very nutritious for vegetarians.

🌹 Decoration

Garnish the bean dish with green peppers.
