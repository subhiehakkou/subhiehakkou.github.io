Kabab Halab Meshwi

The legendary Aleppine kebab: skewers of finely minced lamb seasoned only with black pepper and cinnamon, grilled over hot charcoal. Famous for its juicy texture and the pure, smoky flavor of the meat.

⏲️ Time: 1 hour
🍽️ Servings: The quantity is enough for 4 people.
📱 Open in ASK App Get the full experience — offline timer, voice mode & more

🥄 Ingredients

  • 1 kg very finely ground lamb kebab
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 0.5 tsp cinnamon

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix the kebabs with the seven-spice blend and knead well in a large tray until thoroughly combined before shaping them onto skewers.
  2. Grill onions, tomatoes, and red and green bell peppers (hot or sweet) alongside them.

✨ Chef's Secret

Don't be surprised how the people of Aleppo eat kebabs without a lot of spices; you will be surprised by the distinctive taste of this mixture.

🌹 Decoration

Prepare a dish of onions and parsley, sprinkle with finely chopped red pepper and sumac, and spread it under the kebabs.
