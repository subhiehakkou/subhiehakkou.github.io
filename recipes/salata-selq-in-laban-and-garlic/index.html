Salata Selq in Laban and garlic

A light and refreshing dish of tender chopped Swiss chard mixed with creamy yogurt, garlic, and dried mint. A simple, healthy appetizer served cold.

⏲️ Time: 30 minutes
🍽️ Servings: The quantity as an appetizer is enough for 6 people.
📂 Category: Appetizers
📱 Open in ASK App Get the full experience — offline timer, voice mode & more

🥄 Ingredients

  • When buying chard for stuffing, cut off the tough parts and don't throw them away. Instead, trim and chop them, then make a delicious salad with yogurt and garlic.
  • After washing the chard, boil it thoroughly until it's completely tender. Drain the water and let it cool.
  • 1/2 kg yogurt
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon finely minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon dried mint

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Put the yogurt in a bowl and mix it well with the salt, garlic and dried mint, then add the chard and stir until it is well blended.

✨ Chef's Secret

This innovative dish saves money by using chard stems instead of throwing them away.
