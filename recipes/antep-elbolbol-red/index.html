Antep Elbolbol (Red)

Miniature puff pastry circles topped with a savory blend of minced lamb, finely chopped vegetables (tomatoes and peppers), and pine nuts. A crispy, colorful variation of the traditional bite-sized Aleppine delights.

⏲️ Time: 3 hours
🍽️ Servings: The quantity is enough for 4 people.

🥄 Ingredients

  • 1 kg 30% fat lamb, finely minced
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 bunch parsley (approximately 100 g)
  • 2 tablespoons red pepper paste
  • 1/2 bunch fresh mint (approximately 50 g)
  • 2 small onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon seven-spice mix

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. The dough is the same as the dough for the bird's nest with pomegranate molasses, but as for mixing the meat, put all the ingredients in the minced meat on the smooth side all at once.

✨ Chef's Secret

You can use ready-made phyllo dough or baklava from the market, but it cannot give you the same result as homemade puff pastry dough made by hand. Try both and decide for the future of the bird's nest.

🌹 Decoration

Line a serving dish with lettuce, cucumber, and tomato, then arrange the bird's nest in the center. You can also prepare a cucumber and yogurt side dish or ayran (a yogurt drink). Bird's nest generally pairs well with vegetables such as watercress, green onions, and hot green peppers.
